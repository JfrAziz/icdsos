Strengthening The Contribution of Data Science and Official Statistics To The Society in The Disruption Era

International Conference on Data Science and Official Statistics (ICDSOS) 2021 is organized by Politeknik Statistika STIS and Statistics Indonesia (BPS). This international conference in collaboration with Forum Pendidikan Tinggi Statistika (FORSTAT) and Ikatan Statistisi Indonesia (ISI). The ICDSOS will bring together statisticians and data scientists from academia, official statistics, health sector and business, junior and senior professionals, in an inviting virtual (online) environment on November 13th - 14th, 2021 in regard to the COVID-19 outbreak. Dealing with the theme of this conference is Strengthening The Contribution of Data Science and Official Statistics To The Society in The Disruption Era.
